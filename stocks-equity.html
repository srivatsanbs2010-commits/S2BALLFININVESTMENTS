Stocks & Equity

Investing in individual stocks means buying a piece of a business. This is the primary driver of long-term wealth because equities have historically outperformed almost all other asset classes over decades. However, with high potential reward comes high risk. Successful equity investing requires a deep understanding of company fundamentals, market cycles, and the emotional fortitude to withstand volatility. It is the "growth" component of a portfolio, meant for those with a long-term horizon
