Commodities

Commodities include physical goods like gold, silver, oil, and agricultural products. Gold, in particular, is viewed as a "safe haven" and a hedge against inflation and currency devaluation. Including commodities in a portfolio adds a layer of protection because their prices often rise when the value of paper currency or financial assets falls. They are a "tangible" asset class that provides diversification away from traditional financial instruments.
