Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is the unsung hero of international mobility. It covers a spectrum of risks ranging from the inconvenient (lost baggage or flight delays) to the catastrophic (medical emergencies in foreign countries where healthcare is prohibitively expensive). Many travelers overlook the "Emergency Medical Evacuation" clause, which can cost tens of thousands of dollars out of pocket without coverage. Whether it is for a short vacation or a long-term study abroad program, travel insurance provides peace of mind, ensuring that "getting away from it all" doesn't lead to bringing back a mountain of debt.
