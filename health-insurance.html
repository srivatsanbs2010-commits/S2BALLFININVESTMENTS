Health Insurance

In an era where medical costs are rising faster than general inflation, health insurance is an essential tool for wealth preservation. A single major surgery or a chronic illness can wipe out years of disciplined savings. Health insurance provides a cushion by covering hospitalization expenses, pre- and post-care, and sometimes even outpatient treatments. Key considerations include the sum insured, network hospitals, and waiting periods for pre-existing diseases. Beyond individual plans, "Family Floaters" offer a cost-effective way to cover an entire household. By transferring the risk of high medical bills to an insurance company, individuals can ensure that their long-term investments remain untouched during a health crisis.
