Portfolio Management

Portfolio management is the process of selecting the right mix of the assets mentioned above to meet specific financial goals. It involves Asset Allocation (deciding how much goes into stocks vs. bonds) and Rebalancing (adjusting the mix as market conditions change). A well-managed portfolio isn't about picking the "best" single investment; it's about how different investments work together to maximize returns while minimizing risk.
