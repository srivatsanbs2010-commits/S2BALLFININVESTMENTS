Fixed Deposits

Fixed Deposits remain a favorite for conservative investors. By "locking in" a sum of money for a set tenure at a fixed interest rate, the investor gains absolute certainty. FDs are immune to market volatility, making them ideal for retired individuals or those saving for a short-term goal. While the returns might be lower than equity, the capital protection offered by FDs (often backed by government-guaranteed deposit insurance up to a limit) makes them the bedrock of a balanced portfolio.
